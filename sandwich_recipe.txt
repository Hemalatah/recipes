Ingredients:
 - 1 cup chopped onion
 - 2 slices of multi-grain bread
 - mashed Avocado to stuff b/w the breads
 - lemon to squeeze
 

Directions:
 1. Heat the bread on the tava by applying ghee on both sides.
 
 2. take one slice of bread and stuff the mashed avocado then springle the onion on top of it.

 3. squeeze the lemon as well then place another slice of bread on it to make a sandwich.
